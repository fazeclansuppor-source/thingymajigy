About MacroHub

MacroHub is a creative hub for free macros, built for fun and powered by community sharing. Our mission is to provide high-quality automation tools without the premium paywalls or fake statistics that plague other macro sites. Everything here is genuinely free, created by enthusiasts for enthusiasts.

Founded in 2021 by a group of automation enthusiasts, MacroHub has grown into a thriving community of developers and users who believe in open access to useful tools. Whether you're a gamer looking to automate repetitive tasks or a power user wanting to streamline your workflow, you'll find something useful here.
